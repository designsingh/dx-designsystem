• No data-heavy tables

DLS
Design Leadership Summit — the annual premium conference in Toronto. Bauhaus geometry, editorial typography, maximalist print aesthetic. Dual identity: purple on the DesignX platform, Bauhaus on its own site.
1. Identity
Light mode
Own site: Light/cream (Bauhaus)
Platform signal: Purple #8B5CF6
Mesh: .mesh-dls (light, refined)
Tone: Elevated, editorial, purposeful. Think art exhibition catalog meets design conference. Instrument Serif italic for headlines. Short, considered copy. Premium without pretension.
2. Visual Example
On DesignX Website (Light Section)
Light surfaces, Bauhaus geometry, Instrument Serif headlines, paper grain, halftone dots. Purple as accent color for tags and category signals. Conference content lives here in its natural light mode.
Own Domain (designleadership.io)
Cream parchment, full Bauhaus editorial identity, Instrument Serif headlines, paper grain, halftone dots. The conference's standalone presence — same light aesthetic.

4. Do / Don't
Do

• No rotated blocks
• No fully opaque blocks
• No purple on the own domain
• No dark speaker cards — ever
