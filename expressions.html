Design Rules
SurfaceWarm mode (data-mode="warm"). Cream backgrounds, warm accents.
AccentCoral #E8927C primary. Gold #F59E0B secondary.
Mesh.mesh-community — warm cream base, coral/gold/amber pools
ToneWelcoming, conversational, warm. Never corporate or cold.
HeadlinesDM Sans 700-900, sentence or title case. Less uppercase than Tools.
ProhibitedNo Bauhaus blocks. No dark-only layouts. No data-heavy tables.

DLS
Design Leadership Summit — the annual premium conference in Toronto. Bauhaus geometry, editorial typography, maximalist print aesthetic. Dual identity: purple on the DesignX platform, Bauhaus on its own site.
Identity
data-mode="light"
Own site: Light/cream (Bauhaus)
Platform signal: Purple #8B5CF6
Mesh: .mesh-dls (light, refined)
Dual Identity
On DesignX Platform
Purple gradient glows, dark surfaces. DLS content wears the platform brand. Talk cards, schedule references, speaker mentions all use purple as category signal.
On Own Site (designleadership.io)
Cream parchment, Bauhaus geometry, Instrument Serif headlines, paper grain, halftone dots. No purple. The conference's own editorial identity.
