PHOTOGRAPHY
ON LIGHT

On dark, photos get duotone + halftone. On light, photos stay warmer — soft purple washes, rounded corners, scattered layout. They feel "placed in a scrapbook" not "processed through a pipeline."

Light vs Dark Photography Treatment
DLS '25
Light: Soft, Rotated, Shadowed
Slight desaturation (85%), soft shadow, subtle rotation (±1-3deg). "Pinned to a board" feel. Sticker badges float nearby.
DLS 2025 CREW
Dark: Duotone + Halftone
Full processing pipeline. Grayscale, purple overlay, halftone dots. Text overlay on gradient fade.
Light Photo Collage Layout
WHAT WE BUILT TOGETHER
at DLS 2025
Collage Layout — Slightly Rotated, Soft Shadows
Photos scattered with ±1-3deg rotation, soft drop shadows, slight desaturation. No duotone, no halftone. The warmth of real photography is the point here.
Photography Decision Rules
🌤 Light Mode Photo Rules
Color treatmentSlight desaturation (80-90%). No duotone.
ShapeRounded corners (12-16px). Rotation ±1-3deg.
ShadowSoft drop shadow. 0 6px 24px rgba(26,17,24,0.1).
Overlay textRarely. If needed, use gradient fade from cream.
LayoutScattered/collage. Never grid-locked.
🌙 Dark Mode Photo Rules
Color treatmentDuotone (grayscale → color multiply). Optional halftone.
ShapeSharp corners or 14px radius. Always flat, no rotation.
ShadowNone visible (dark bg absorbs). Glow optional.
Overlay textCommon. Use gradient fade from black.
LayoutGrid-locked. Systematic. Card-based.
