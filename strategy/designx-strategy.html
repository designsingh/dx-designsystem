01 · Foundation

System
Architecture

Two brands. One ecosystem. DesignX is the platform — dark, systematic, tool-oriented. DLS (Design Leadership Summit) is the conference brand — light, maximalist, editorial. They share DNA but express it completely differently.

🌙 DesignX (Platform)

The professional home for design leaders building with AI. Dark UI, systematic grid, glass effects. Houses tools: Salary, Jobs, The Stack, Archetypes.

The Golden Rule

Content wears the brand of where it's displayed.
A DLS talk on designleadership.io wears light Bauhaus. The same talk on app.designx wears dark platform. The content doesn't change — the presentation layer does.

One exception: DLS speaker cards and Bauhaus visual assets are always light — they ARE the content, not a wrapper.

02 · Visual Language

Gradient
Spectrum

The existing gradient library spans the full emotional range between the two brands. Rather than a hard binary switch (dark/light), these gradients create a continuous spectrum from pure DesignX through transitional zones to pure DLS. Each product world occupies a position on this spectrum.

The Five Zones

Map the gradient library into five zones. Each zone serves a different brand context, and each product world gravitates toward a home zone.

Pure DesignX DarkDark + ColorTool WorldsWarm BridgePure DLS Light
Zone 1
Pure Dark
Deep purple-black. Core DesignX UI, nav, backgrounds.
Zone 2
Dark + Vibrant
Purple/blue/neon on dark. Tool heroes, DLS purple cards, salary neon.
Zone 3
Tool Worlds
Green, blue, orange on dark. Product-specific gradients. Maximum color contrast.
Zone 4
Warm Bridge
Coral, amber, warm. Archetype territory. Transitional zone toward light.
Zone 5
Pure Light
Cream, paper, warm white. DLS editorial, Bauhaus blocks on top.
How Products Map to Zones
DesignX UI / Nav / ShellZone 1 — Pure dark gradients
DLS on platform (event cards, ticket CTA)Zone 2 — Purple-dominant dark
Salary ToolZone 2-3 — Neon green on deep dark
Job BoardZone 3 — Blue/cyan on dark
CommunityZone 3-4 — Gold/warm on dark
Archetypes (on platform)Zone 4 — Warm amber, bridges to light
Archetypes (standalone/DLS)Zone 4-5 — Each archetype's own color on cream/paper
DLS editorial / designleadership.ioZone 5 — Pure light, Bauhaus blocks on cream
Using the Gradient Library

Selection rule: Pick gradients from the zone that matches your content's position on the spectrum. A Salary tool hero pulls from Zone 2-3 (neon/green family). A DLS speaker card backdrop pulls from Zone 5 (warm/light family). The Archetypes reveal moment can pull from Zone 4 (warm bridge) because it's the brand that literally bridges both worlds.

05 · DLS Conference

Light System
Quick Reference

The essentials for any DLS editorial asset. Full specs in the DLS Dual Mode v2 document. The light system is maximalist Bauhaus collage — the opposite of DesignX's minimal dark.

The 6 Ingredients
① Bauhaus Blocks
Overlapping colored rectangles + circles. Never rotated. Full spectrum. 6-12 per speaker card, 3-6 for smaller uses. 30-50% overlap.
② B&W Halftone
Grayscale → contrast(1.4) → halftone dot (3.5px grid). On light: sits on colored bg with Bauhaus visible behind.
③ Text Wallpaper
"Speaker Speaker Speaker" repeating at angles. Instrument Serif italic, 28-48px, 3-6% opacity. Multi-color.
④ Paper Grain
35-45% opacity. Visible fiber texture. Trading card / zine cutout feel. Covers everything.
⑤ Serif Headlines
Instrument Serif is THE headline voice. Speaker names, talk titles, section headings, archetype names. DM Sans for body only.
⑥ Multi-Color
Full Bauhaus spectrum: red, yellow, green, blue, orange, coral, purple. Speaker card backgrounds: olive, coral, amber, forest, teal.
DLS Card Types
Speaker cardColored bg + Bauhaus blocks + halftone portrait + "Speaker" wallpaper + serif name
Talk title cardCream base + "Interactive Session" serif pill + serif title + halftone speakers in blocks
Sponsor/Patron cardRainbow marquee text border all 4 sides + cream center + "Patrons" in serif + Bauhaus cluster

Design Rules

On the dark platform: Archetypes follow the same card language as Salary and Jobs — canvas bg, gradient border accent, DM Sans headlines, glass sections. No Bauhaus blocks, no paper grain, no watermark text. The archetype name appears in Instrument Serif italic ONLY on the reveal moment.

On DLS / standalone: Full Bauhaus card treatment. Lanyard format, archetype color full-bleed, Toronto illustration, serif name, paper grain, watermark text. This is the conference badge format.
