Light System
Visual Reference

Maximalist Bauhaus collage. The complete opposite of DesignX's minimal dark. Every DLS asset needs these 6 ingredients.

The 6 Ingredients
① Bauhaus Blocks
Overlapping rectangles + circles. Never rotated. Full color spectrum. 6-12 per speaker card.
② B&W Halftone
Grayscale → contrast 1.4 → halftone 3.5px dots. Sits on colored bg with Bauhaus visible behind.
③ Text Wallpaper
"Speaker Speaker Speaker" repeating. Instrument Serif italic, 3-6% opacity. Multi-color.
④ Paper Grain
35-45% opacity. Visible fiber texture. Trading card / zine cutout feel.
⑤ Serif Headlines
Instrument Serif IS the headline voice. Speaker names, talk titles, section headings.
⑥ Multi-Color
Red, yellow, green, blue, orange, coral, purple. Speaker bg: olive, coral, amber, forest, teal.
