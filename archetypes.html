Leadership Archetypes
Archetype
System
Five leadership archetypes with branded visual assets — reveal moments, share cards, and conference lanyards. Content born at DLS, living across both brands.
Archetype Cards
Support · Enablement · Trust
The Ally
Amplifies others. Builds bridges. Creates psychological safety.
Systems · Orchestration · Leverage
The Architect
Designs systems. Sees connections. Scales through structure.
Exploration · Vision · First-Principles
The Pathfinder
Charts new territory. Questions assumptions. Leads through vision.
Stability · Depth · Resilience
The Anchor
Holds the center. Absorbs uncertainty. Provides calm in chaos.
People · Culture · Growth
The Cultivator
Grows teams. Nurtures talent. Builds culture as competitive advantage.
