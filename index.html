Brand Positioning
Before the colors, typography, and components — understand who DesignX is, who it serves, and why it exists.
Who We Are
DesignX is a platform and community for design leaders. We build tools, host events, and create spaces where senior designers grow into better leaders — through data, shared experience, and honest conversation.
Who It's For
Design managers, directors, and VPs who've outgrown tutorials and portfolios. People navigating org charts, comp negotiations, team scaling, and the loneliness of leading creative teams. Practitioners first, titles second.
Why We Exist
Design leadership has no playbook. No salary benchmarks, no career ladders, no peer community at scale. DesignX exists to fix that — with transparency, data, and a conference that treats design leaders like the executives they are.

The Golden Rule
Content wears the brand of its section.
DesignX is one website with three sections, each in its natural mode. DLS conference content lives in the light section. Tools live in the dark section. Community lives in the warm section. The content adapts to its home.

Think of it like rooms in one building — each room has its own lighting, but they share the same architecture.

Exception: DLS speaker announcement cards are ALWAYS light Bauhaus, even when promoted elsewhere. The Bauhaus card IS the content — it's a visual asset, not a text post wearing a wrapper.
Key Principles
Community Events ≠ Mini-DLS
Meetups, design dinners, and local gatherings are casual, community-vibe events. They're NOT smaller versions of the conference. Warm, approachable, informal — always on the warm surface.
No Bauhaus on Dark
Bauhaus geometry only appears on light/cream surfaces. Dark sections use glass, grain, and gradients. Instrument Serif on dark is for reveal moments and salary figures only.
Color Worlds = Content Signatures
Color worlds identify content by type. Tools each get a single signature color (green, blue, crimson). DLS has the full Bauhaus palette — it's a system, not one color. Community uses coral-gold warmth.
